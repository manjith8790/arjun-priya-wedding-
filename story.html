Our Story

🤵
Arjun Sharma
The Groom

A software engineer by profession and an adventure seeker at heart, Arjun grew up in T. Nagar, Chennai. He believes the best conversations happen over filter kaapi and long ECR drives. When he's not coding, you'll find him on hiking trails or quietly playing the mridangam — a talent he never mentions first.


He is the kind of person who shows up — for friends, for family, and for the people he loves. Ask anyone who knows him, and they'll tell you: Arjun makes everything feel a little easier.

· · · ✦ · · ·
Priya Krishnamurthy
The Bride

A passionate architect who designs spaces that breathe, Priya brings the same thoughtfulness to every relationship she nurtures. Born and raised in Mylapore, she's the person who remembers everyone's favourite dessert and shows up with it unannounced on a hard day.


She met Arjun at a mutual friend's wedding — she was fixing the decorator's mistakes; he was watching her with quiet admiration. She noticed eventually. The rest is the story you're here to celebrate.

How It Started

"They said the best love stories feel like coming home — and with you, I am finally, irrevocably home."

What began as a chance meeting at a wedding in 2022 slowly became late-night calls, shared playlists, and the quiet certainty that this was it. Three years later, they're writing their own vows.
